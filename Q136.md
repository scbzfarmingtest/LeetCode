## Exclusive OR
If the requirements of an integer question cannot be all satisfied, especially when time and space limitations are much too strict, it is worthy to consider ***EXCLUSIVE OR***.

**Property of *EXCLUSIVE OR:***
- Associativity: $(A\oplus B) \oplus C= A\oplus (B\oplus C)$
- Commutative: $A\oplus B=B\oplus A$
- Falsehood-preserving: $0\oplus 0\oplus ... \oplus 0=0$
- <u>**Without**</u> truth-preserving: 
  - $1\oplus 1=0$  
  - $1\oplus 1\oplus 1=0$
